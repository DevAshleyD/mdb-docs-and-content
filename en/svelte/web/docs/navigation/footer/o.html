Svelte Footer - Bootstrap 4 & Material Design

Svelte Bootstrap Footer is an additional navigation for the website. It can hold links, buttons, company info, copyrights, forms and many other elements.

You can set the color of the footer by adding one of the classes from our color palette.

Just like any other componentd of MDBootstrap Svelte, Footers are responsive by default.
